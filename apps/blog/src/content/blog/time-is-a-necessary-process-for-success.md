#startup #accelerator-applications

In chemistry, there's multiple ways to have two compounds react with each other. Even though, there's multiple ways to [accelerate reactions](/blog/speeding-up-chemical-reactions), time is always a factor.

Startups need to accept that  there's a minimal lower bound for success to happen, but at the same time should be extremely impatient to find ways to accelerate the reaction.
